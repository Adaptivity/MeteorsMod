Falling Meteors Mod

Author: AlexDGr8r

This mod for Minecraft adds meteors that fall down from the sky. With these meteors come
various types of minerals and ores to create new weapons, tools, and armor from. Also
adds new tools for tracking down meteors as well as blocking them from falling on to
your creations.

Minecraft Forums Link: http://www.minecraftforum.net/topic/989113-147sspsmpfalling-meteors-modv292/
