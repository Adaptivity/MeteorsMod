Welcome!

This mod for Minecraft adds meteors that fall down from the sky. With these meteors come various types of minerals and ores to create new weapons, tools, and armor from. Also adds new tools for tracking down meteors as well as blocking them from falling on to your creations.

Check out the Wiki

The wiki holds (or will hold rather) all the information about the mod. This includes, crafting recipes, guides, as well as support info.
